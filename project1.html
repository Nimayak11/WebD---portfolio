The best movies according to Navya

My top 3 movies of all-time

The Godfather

I am not really into movies but this 4 hour flick kept me hooked throughout! Real and the direction was quite impactful.

3 idiots

Probably an all India faourite, this movie took away my heart with its simple yet strong script that really hit home. The actors were fabulous as well.

Amar prem

I have a whole lot of childhood memories linked to this movie. Plus, the storyline is an easy 8.5/10. Rajesh Khanna,the male protagonist was one of the top Indian actors that I really admire (I was a huge fan!!). We had bought a CD that had this and 2 other movies stored in it (timeline - 2000s) and I remember rewatching them with my parents atleast once every year, lol. Can't imagine rewatching a movie anymore though. It's just not my thing.
